With Liselotte’s assistance (after revealing her past life), Rio and Miharu gain an audience with Sumeragi Satsuki ahead of the banquet. Once preparations are complete, Rio and company head to the capital of Galtuuk to finally meet the hero named Satsuki. Miharu and Satsuki are ecstatic to reunite with one another, but the tables turn when Rio discloses his plans for the future...





The banquet full of complex emotions begins now!
